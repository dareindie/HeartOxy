# HeartOxy

**Train smarter in your target heart rate zone — with real-time intensity feedback on your wrist.**

HeartOxy helps you start structured Apple Watch workouts and train in your selected heart rate zone with real-time intensity feedback and clear post-workout summaries.

Whether you're warming up, targeting fat burn, or pushing aerobic performance, HeartOxy keeps your session focused and zone-based.

---

## Key Features

- Start structured Apple Watch workouts
- Select your preferred heart rate zone (Warm-up, Fat Burn, Aerobic, Anaerobic, or Custom)
- Real-time intensity feedback during your workout
- Live zone indicator with colour-coded display on your wrist
- Clear visual indicators for Recovery, Low, Target, High, and Peak intensity levels
- Post-workout summary with heart rate stats (Avg / Max / Min)
- Zone distribution breakdown after every session
- Customisable intensity thresholds via sliders
- Session history and workout tracking on iPhone
- CSV export for deeper personal analysis
- Data syncs automatically from Watch to iPhone during monitoring

---

## How It Works

1. Open HeartOxy on your Apple Watch
2. Choose your target heart rate zone on iPhone (Settings)
3. Slide to start your workout
4. Train with live intensity feedback on your wrist
5. Review your session summary and zone distribution when done

---

## Designed for Apple Watch

HeartOxy is built specifically for Apple Watch users who want simple, structured heart rate zone training without complicated dashboards.

All workouts are user-initiated and focused on active exercise sessions. HeartOxy uses Apple Watch workout sessions to record fitness data and provide structured zone-based training feedback — workouts are saved to Apple Health and visible in the Fitness app.

---

## Heart Rate Zones

HeartOxy calculates personalised heart rate zones based on your age:

| Zone | Name | % of Max HR |
|------|------|-------------|
| Zone 1 | Warm-up | 60 – 70% |
| Zone 2 | Fat Burn | 70 – 80% |
| Zone 3 | Aerobic | 80 – 90% |
| Zone 4 | Anaerobic | 90 – 100% |
| Custom | Custom | User-defined |

|--------|---------|--------------|

> Apple Watch Series 4, 5, and SE support heart rate monitoring only. SpO₂ readings will show as unavailable on these models. Series 3 and below are not supported.
